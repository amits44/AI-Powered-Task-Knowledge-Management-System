EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Future Trans Corporation delivered strong performance in Q2 2026, reporting a
net revenue of $4.82 million, reflecting a 14.3% year-over-year growth compared
to Q2 2025. Operating expenses were well-controlled, resulting in a net profit
margin of 22.6%. The company continues to expand its client base across
translation and localization services.
